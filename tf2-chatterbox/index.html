TF2 ChatterBox

By RalphORama (/u/ralphorama)

Follow the instructions to have a good time!
  1. Enter each phrase you want the bind to rotate through below.
    • Only enter one phrase per line.
    • Avoid the use of the quote (") character.
  2. Name your bind using the "Bind Name" box.
  3. Choose whether your bind display messages at random.
  4. If you'd like, add a value for the key to trigger your bind.
  5. Disable the watermark if you like.
  6. Click "Generate!"
  7. Copy the contents of the lower text field into your autoexec.cfg (or any other cfg file) in your steamapps/common/team fortress 2/tf/cfg folder.
  8. Spam away happily!
